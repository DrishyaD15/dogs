Ups and Downs of getting a dog

Go back to the main page

Advantage

A good thing about getting a dog is that you will have a companion throughout 10-15 years of your life (maybe more maybe less who knows?). That dog will be the most loyal thing you know.You will never be lonely and have a dog to keep you company. That dog will be like your emotional support and love you to the moon. Also getting a dog has been proven to cause less stress, anxiety, and loneliness

Disandvantge

A disadvantage of having a dog is that for maybe 2 years of its life (if you get a puppy) it will be in its "bratty age". Even if you train it, 6 months-2yrs the dog will be super bratty. Those years it will be hard but don't punish the dog. This is just a phase it goes through. But it will never learn if you treat it like a baby.

Advantage

A good thing about getting a dog is that you will be more physically fit. Because dogs require lots of exercise, you will also have to play, walk, or run with the dog. Simply walking your dog will provide good exercise.

Disandvantge

A disadvantage about getting a dog is that they aren't free. Vet bills, toys, grooming, food, and supplies for your dog in general. But at least you will have something that you take care of. But if you are ready to spoil your dog with love then this is a minor disadvantage.

In all this is good and bad things about getting a dog

Once you get a dog will you have a bunch of love in your house? YES OFCOURSE. Will your house smell like a dog? YES. But will you have a parade when you get back home? YES.
